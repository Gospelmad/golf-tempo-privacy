Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

Golf Tempo Trainer ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices for the App.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not share data with third parties
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not require internet connectivity

Local Storage

The App stores minimal preferences locally on your device only, including:

  • Whether you have seen the welcome message
  • No other data is stored

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Vibration - To provide haptic feedback when buttons are pressed
  • Foreground Service - To keep audio tempo playing when the app is in the background

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not enable data collection.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or social media integrations.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no data to secure beyond what is stored locally on your device by the Android operating system.
